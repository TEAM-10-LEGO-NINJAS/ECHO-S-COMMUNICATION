What is ECHO'S

The Hand Gesture Recognition system is a widely used technology for helping disabled people . The general definition of gesture recognition is the ability of a computer to understand gestures and execute commands based on those gestures. The idea behind this system is to understand sign language so that disabled people can communicate with the outside world without any interpreter.. There are a few systems available for sign language to speech conversion but none of them provide a portable user interface.
